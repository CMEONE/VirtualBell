About the Harker Middle School Virtual Bell

By: Grant Yang <25granty@students.harker.org>
    This virtual bell application was written to help students and teachers schedule their zoom meetings at home in the absence of the bell (COVID-19 closure). It follows the official middle school schedule posted by Mr. Barth. You can find the zoom links and other useful information at the middle school google doc. This website is completely open-source at https://gituhb.com/ROTARTSI82/VirtualBell, and it uses bootstrap. It is inspired by Dr. Linton's WhatNow app. Whatever you do, don't use the bare-bones "Official" bell made by Kabir. If you find a bug (which is pretty likely), please report it by opening an issue.
